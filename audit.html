What we analyse

4 areas that make or break your website

First impression

Does your homepage immediately communicate what you do and why you're the right choice? We assess visual hierarchy, headline clarity and brand consistency.

  • Hero message clarity
  • Visual identity consistency
  • Trust signals above the fold

Mobile experience

Over 70% of Singapore web traffic is mobile. We test your site on small screens and flag every layout, touch target or readability issue.

  • Responsive layout audit
  • Touch target sizing
  • Mobile loading speed

Speed & performance

A 1-second delay costs you 7% of conversions. We identify the top issues slowing your site down — images, fonts, render-blocking scripts — and tell you exactly what to fix.

  • Core Web Vitals review
  • Image optimisation gaps
  • Script & CSS load order

Conversion & calls to action

Visitors land on your site — then what? We review every call-to-action, contact path and lead capture point to find where you're losing potential customers.

  • CTA visibility & copy
  • Contact friction analysis
  • Booking / enquiry flow
